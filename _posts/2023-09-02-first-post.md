## Welcome!

Hello there and welcome to my tech blog written by a human (that's me)! I wanted to give a brief introduction of myself and what I plan on posting here.

### Who I Am

My name is Brittan Reichley, and I am currently a Site Reliability Engineer working mostly with cloud platforms as well as on-premise systems. I enjoy learning about various technology topics including (but not limited to) software development, cybersecurity, cloud infrastructure, and automation. I'm a big DevSecOps enthusiast with the dream of eliminating all of the silos we have between teams in the industry today.

### What To Blog About?

My goal for this blog is to focus on documenting new technologies as I begin tinkering with them as well as a way to document/solidify the topics and skills I already have. While it will help myself reference things, I also hope my posts may help others and generate conversations to improve my own and others understandings of how things work. Maybe there's a better way of approaching a problem? Perhaps something wasn't described the way it was supposed to be? We can only grow by having civilized, productive, and constructive conversations, which I aim to faciliate here for everyone.

As I am a human being, there may also be some (what I would consider) fun posts popping up from time to time. Topics like recipes I have made, fun travel locations I've gone, and the occasional book/video game review. I feel it's never a bad thing to faciliate a good work to life balance as there's a whole world out there to experience (and we only have short time here after all 😉).

### Conclusion

To conclude this post, I appreciate anyone who may have stopped by to read this, and I hope you may stick around for the future content I plan on creating. Feel free to interact with any of my content or reach out with any inquiries. I'll try to respond as soon as I can!
